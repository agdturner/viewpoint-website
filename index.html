VIEWpoint and CSSP-China

The VIEWpoint project seeks to maximise the impact of the world-class collaborative scientific research of the CSSP-China (Climate Science for Service Partnership China) programme. VIEWpoint has created tools, demonstrators, articles and videos to make research outputs more accessible to a wider audience, including government, industry and the public.

“新观点”(VIEWpoint)项目旨在最大化地发挥“中英气候科学到服务伙伴关系计划”(CSSP-China: Climate Science for Service Partnership China)的世界级科研合作的影响力。通过创建工具、演示、文章和视频，“新观点”项目让包括政府、企业和公众等在内的不同群体更易获得研究成果。
